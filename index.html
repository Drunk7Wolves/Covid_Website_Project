    • Create a user friendly interface

    Background Information

    COVID-19 is a respiratory virus that was spread throughout the world primarily in 2020 and onwards. COVID-19 was first discovered in late 2019 in China. Some common symptoms include:

    • Fever
    • Cough
    • Fatigue
    • Trouble Breathing
    • Loss of Taste or Smell
